Data Deletion: Upon account deletion, all personal data associated with your account, including your profile information, activity logs, will be permanently removed from our systems.

Data Retention: Some data, such as records required for legal compliance, may be retained for up to 7 years after account deletion, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
